- [Ascend Device Plugin](https://www.hiascend.com/document/detail/zh/mindcluster/2600/clustersched/dlug/docs/zh/scheduling/installation_guide/03_installation/manual_installation/03_npu_exporter.md)
- [Valcano](https://www.hiascend.com/document/detail/zh/mindcluster/2600/clustersched/dlug/docs/zh/scheduling/installation_guide/03_installation/manual_installation/05_volcano.md)
- [ClusterD](https://www.hiascend.com/document/detail/zh/mindcluster/2600/clustersched/dlug/docs/zh/scheduling/installation_guide/03_installation/manual_installation/06_clusterd.md)
- [NodeD](https://www.hiascend.com/document/detail/zh/mindcluster/2600/clustersched/dlug/docs/zh/scheduling/installation_guide/03_installation/manual_installation/09_noded.md)
